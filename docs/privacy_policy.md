# Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-02-06

This Privacy Policy explains how WealthPulse ("App") collects and uses information.

## 1. Who We Are
WealthPulse is developed by **L P Scott - ScottWebDesign**.
Contact: **wealthpulse@outlook.co.nz**

## 2. Data We Collect
Depending on your settings, we may collect:
- Account data (username, password hash, recovery questions)
- Portfolio data (assets, liabilities, notes)
- Community data (listings, messages, images)
- Usage data (basic analytics to improve the app)

## 3. How We Use Data
We use data to:
- Provide core app functionality
- Sync data between devices (if enabled)
- Improve the app experience
- Respond to support requests

## 4. Where Data Is Stored
You can use the app in local-only mode, or enable cloud storage (e.g., Supabase). If cloud storage is enabled, data may be stored on third-party services you configure.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with third-party services when required to provide functionality you enable (e.g., live pricing APIs).

## 6. Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding access to your device and account.

## 7. Your Rights
You may request to export or delete your data. Contact **wealthpulse@outlook.co.nz** for assistance.

## 8. Changes
We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use means you accept updates.
